SIA Consultation Flowchart

A clear roadmap for executing a Social Impact Assessment (SIA) and achieving verified outcomes.

Phase 1 (Policy)

Foundation & Scoping

Define the project scope and boundaries. Ensure alignment with existing governance and policy mandates.

Milestone: Scoping Document Approval

Phase 2 (Assurance)

Data Collection & Baseline

Gather primary and secondary data. Engage stakeholders to establish the project's 'but-for' case and initial metrics.

Milestone: Data Validation Sign-off

Phase 3 (Financing)

Impact Analysis & Valuation

Apply SROI and other methodologies to quantify and assign a social value to the identified changes and outcomes.

Milestone: Calculated Impact Summary Review

Phase 4 (Assurance)

Final Reporting & Verification

Finalize the report and compile evidence for external assurance (e.g., SVI verification) before publication.

Milestone: Independent Assurance Statement

Phase 5 (Policy/Financing)

Strategy Integration & M&E

Integrate verified findings into policy, procurement, and future investment decisions. Develop long-term monitoring.

Milestone: Implementation Roadmap Finalized
